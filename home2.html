About

Conservation of Nature

Our overall goal is conservation of nature. We consider nature as the creator, and supreme in beauty and health.To achieve this we will strive to build a sustainable economy and environment, and theirby conserve natural resources in its original, pristine beauty. In this process we will promote : Reduce, ReUse , ReCycling and Re-newables; and demote over-construction, over-production,and over-use of resouces.


Our Organization

The organization is based on principles of: non violence, not corrupt , discipline, freedom of expression, and unity through equal treatment (justice for all) .The members and leaders (elected) will function on these principles to achieve our goals.The key strength of the organization is it's members, and the leaders shall be representatives of the members. Leaders are elected (except the founder, all others are elected) fairly, for a limited term of 5 years , and will operate in a sense of obligation to nature. Transparency and access of information shall be ensured in all levels.
